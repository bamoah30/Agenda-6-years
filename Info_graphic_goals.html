The 6-Year Phased Journey

This journey is a structured progression from fundamental principles to frontier research and industry leadership, broken into distinct, overlapping phases.

Phase 1-2: Foundations (Weeks 1-36)

Building the bedrock. Mastering core Python, electronics fundamentals, C++, and the fundamentals of Calculus, Linear Algebra, and classical Machine Learning.

Phase 3-4: Core Skills (Weeks 37-92)

Assembling the toolkit. Deep dives into Transformer architecture, MLOps, sensor fusion, advanced control theory (PID, LQR), and robot kinematics.

Phase 5: System Integration (Weeks 93-156)

Making it work together. Mastering ROS, system-scale design, distributed training for LLMs, and integrating perception (CV/LiDAR) with manipulation (MoveIt!).

Phase 6: Frontier Research (Weeks 157-276)

Pushing the boundaries. Contributing to frontier AI research, scaling laws, advanced SLAM, dexterous manipulation, and building ChatGPT-scale projects.

Phase 7: Leadership (Weeks 277-312)

Deep specialization in a chosen track (e.g., Autonomous Vehicles, AI Safety), publishing research, and establishing industry expertise as a team lead or founder.

Pillar 1: The AI Journey
